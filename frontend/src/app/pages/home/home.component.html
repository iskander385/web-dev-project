About the Show

Supernatural is an American dark fantasy television series that aired on The WB and later The CW from September 13, 2005 to November 19, 2020. Created by Eric Kripke, the show follows brothers Sam and Dean Winchester as they travel across America in a 1967 Chevrolet Impala hunting demons, ghosts, monsters, and other supernatural beings.

Over its 15 seasons, the show expanded its mythology to include angels, demons, gods from multiple pantheons, and even God himself. What began as a monster-of-the-week horror series evolved into an epic story about free will, family, sacrifice, and the ultimate battle between good and evil.
